Golden Flake

Indredients

  • Pizza base & sauce
  • Cheese
  • Mixed veggies

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  3. Add a generous amount of cheese.
  4. Top with your favorite veggies.
  5. Drizzle a little olive oil and season with oregano.
  6. Bake for 10–15 mins until cheese melts & golden.
  7. Serve hot!

Dosa Delight

Indredients

  • Ghee & Coconut
  • Tamarind and spices
  • Rice and urad dal
  • Green chilies and coriander

Recipe

  1. Soak rice and urad dal, grind to a smooth paste,
    and ferment overnight.
  2. Spread batter on a hot pan, drizzle with oil,
    and cook until crispy.
  3. Make coconut chutney, green chutney, and sambar.
  4. Serve with a sprinkle of chopped coriander or
    curry leaves for added aroma.
  5. Serve dosa hot with chutneys and sambar.

Crispy Samosas

Indredients

  • All-purpose flour (for dough)
  • Potatoes, peas, and spices (for filling)
  • Oil (for frying)

Recipe

  1. Prepare Dough: Knead a soft dough with flour, salt,
    and water.
  2. Make Filling: Cook mashed potatoes, peas,
    and spices.
  3. Shape Samosas: Roll dough, fill with the potato mixture,
    and fold into a triangular shape.
  4. Seal Edges: Use water to seal the edges tightly to
    prevent opening during frying.
  5. Fry: Deep fry until golden brown and crispy.

Classic Sandwich

Indredients

  • toasted Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mashed Potato mix
  • Tomato slices & Lettuce

Recipe

  1. Spread mayonnaise on toasted bread slices.
  2. Layer with lettuce, tomatoes, and another slice of bread.
  3. Repeat layers with potato mix and secure with toothpicks.
  4. Lightly grill the sandwich for extra crunch and flavor.
  5. Cut diagonally and serve with chips or fries.
